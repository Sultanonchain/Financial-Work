Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 4, 2026

WE DO NOT SELL YOUR DATA. PERIOD.

1. Our Core Belief

ValusFinancial was built on the principles of financial transparency, individual sovereignty, and personal privacy. We believe your financial data, research habits, and investment interests belong to you — and only you. We will never sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal data to any third party, advertiser, data broker, hedge fund, or government entity. This is not a loophole-filled promise — it is an absolute commitment.

2. What We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the platform:

  • Pages and tools you visit on our site
  • Stocks, tickers, or financial instruments you search or analyze
  • Device type and browser (for performance optimization only)
  • Approximate location (country/region level only, via Vercel Analytics)
  • Account information if you create an account (email, username)

3. What We Will NEVER Do

  • Sell your data to data brokers, advertisers, or third parties
  • Share your stock research or financial interests with hedge funds or financial institutions
  • Use your browsing behavior to target you with ads
  • Build psychological profiles on you for commercial gain
  • Share your personal information with government agencies without a valid legal order
  • Use deceptive opt-outs or dark patterns to trick you into sharing more than you intend

4. Analytics & Tracking

We use Vercel Web Analytics to understand how our platform is being used. This tool is privacy-focused by design — it does not use cookies, does not track individuals across sessions, and does not share data with advertising networks. The data we see is aggregated (e.g., "500 visitors from the US this week") — never tied to individual identities.

5. Future Brokerage Integrations (Robinhood, SoFi, etc.)

In the future, ValusFinancial may offer integrations with brokerage platforms such as Robinhood or SoFi to allow users to execute trades directly. If and when these features are introduced:

  • You will be fully informed before connecting any brokerage account
  • Brokerage credentials will NEVER be stored on our servers
  • OAuth-based authentication will be used so we never see your passwords
  • Trade data accessed through integrations will only be used to provide the service you requested — never analyzed, sold, or shared
  • You can revoke access to any brokerage integration at any time

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Our infrastructure runs on Vercel, which maintains SOC 2 compliance. We apply the principle of least privilege — our systems only access the data they absolutely need to function.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of any optional data collection at any time
  • Know exactly what data we have collected and why

ValusFinancial.com is a purely educational platform designed to help users learn about financial concepts such as Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) analysis, stock valuation, and market research. Nothing on this site — including but not limited to tools, calculators, data, analysis, summaries, or any other content — constitutes financial advice, investment advice, trading advice, or any recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security or financial instrument.

We are not registered investment advisors, broker-dealers, financial planners, or licensed financial professionals of any kind. The information provided on this platform is for general educational and informational purposes only.

Any financial decisions you make are entirely your own responsibility. Always do your own research (DYOR). Before making any investment decisions, we strongly encourage you to consult with a qualified, licensed financial professional who understands your personal financial situation.

Past performance of any stock, asset, or financial instrument discussed on this platform does not guarantee future results. All investing involves risk, including the potential loss of principal.
